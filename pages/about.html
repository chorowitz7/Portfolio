About Me

My journey started off as a student at the University of Maine in 2016 as a Finance student. After completing a FinTech course during my Spring 2017 semester, I realized that going into the technology field is what I wanted to pursue. Building coupled with technology is the perfect combination to fuel my passion for the web development and software development field.

Technology is the now and the future, and I want to be a part of the journey.
